CEL, BITS Goa

The Centre for Entrepreneurial Leadership (CEL) at BITS Goa is a hub for innovation and entrepreneurship. We foster creativity, leadership, and business acumen in students, preparing them for the challenges of the startup world.

Who Are We?

CEL is a student-run organization dedicated to promoting entrepreneurship and fostering leadership skills among BITS Goa students. We organize workshops, seminars, and competitions to provide hands-on experience in the startup ecosystem.

What We Do

Our activities include organizing entrepreneurship workshops, hosting startup competitions, providing mentorship programs, and facilitating networking events with industry experts. We aim to create a vibrant entrepreneurial community on campus.
